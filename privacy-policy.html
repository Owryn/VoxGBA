Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Voxura Studios ("we", "our", "us") built VoxGBA - Retro Emulator ("the App") as a freemium application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the App.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All game data, save states, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob — We display ads in the free version of the App. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your consent. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Play Billing — If you purchase the Pro upgrade, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information. Google Privacy Policy

Advertising & Consent

We use Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to obtain your consent before showing personalized ads, in compliance with GDPR and other applicable regulations. You can change your ad preferences at any time through the consent form or your device settings.

Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to us:

  • Game ROM files you load into the App
  • Save states and game progress
  • App settings and preferences (controls, display, audio)
  • Play time statistics
  • Cheat code selections

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App's target audience is users aged 18 and above.

Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy. All app data remains on your device under your control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
